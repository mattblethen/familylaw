What Happens Next

Three steps that help bring shape to an uncertain situation.

The goal of the first consultation is not to pressure you. It is to understand what is happening, explain your options, and help you decide what to do next.

1

Start With The Facts

We talk through what is happening, what concerns feel most urgent, and what deadlines or practical issues need attention first.

2

Review Your Options

You get a clearer understanding of the legal landscape, the likely paths ahead, and the decisions that matter most.

3

Choose A Plan Forward

From there, we can move into negotiation, mediation, or litigation with a better sense of purpose and preparation.

Preparation

Good preparation makes every next step more manageable.

Knowing what documents matter, what questions to ask, and what timeline you may be facing can make a difficult situation feel more structured and less overwhelming.
