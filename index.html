FAQ

Questions prospective clients often ask before they reach out.

What happens in the first consultation?

The consultation is a chance to explain what is going on, understand the immediate legal issues, and leave with a clearer sense of options and next steps.

Do I have to be sure I am ready to file before I call?

No. Many people reach out while they are still gathering information and trying to understand what a legal process might involve.

Can family-law matters be resolved outside of court?

Some matters can be resolved through negotiation or mediation, while others require a stronger litigation posture. The right path depends on the facts and the people involved.

Do you handle cases involving children?

Yes. The starter is built around custody, parenting plans, support matters, and related family-law concerns where children are central to the case.
